## This Site's Purpose
Clinical alcohol detox and withdrawal reference page.

The page is designed for people trying to understand what
alcohol detox involves, which withdrawal symptoms may occur,
how symptoms can develop over time and when professional or
urgent medical support is needed.

It also explains how detox connects to rehab, but the core
subject is physical withdrawal and safe stabilisation rather
than choosing between broad rehab providers.

## This Site's Content Angle
This site owns the detailed alcohol withdrawal and detox
reference angle.

Its main content areas are:

- What alcohol detox means
- Why alcohol withdrawal happens
- Who may need medical supervision
- Mild, moderate and severe withdrawal symptoms
- The alcohol withdrawal timeline
- Risks such as seizures and delirium tremens
- Home, hospital and residential detox settings
- Detox versus rehab
- How detox should connect into therapy and aftercare

The distinctive angle is symptom severity, timing and medical
risk. Rehab appears mainly as the next stage after physical
stabilisation.
