Writing Principles
Speak directly
Use "you" and "we".
Good
You don't have to face this alone.
We're here to support you through every stage of recovery.
Avoid
Individuals suffering from addiction may benefit from professional intervention.

Reassure first
Many visitors are anxious, frightened or uncertain.
Lead with reassurance.
Good
We understand that taking the first step can feel difficult.
Your information will remain completely confidential.
Our friendly team will simply listen and guide you through your options.
Avoid
Contact us immediately for treatment.

Use simple language
Write in plain English.
Explain medical or recovery concepts in everyday language.
Good
Detox is the process of safely removing alcohol or drugs from the body under medical supervision.
Avoid
Detoxification involves the elimination of toxic substances and physiological stabilisation.
________________


Speak with confidence, not hype
Be professional and reassuring.
Never exaggerate.
Good
We provide expert care and personalised support.
We offer evidence-based treatment and therapies.
Avoid
The UK's leading rehab clinic.
Guaranteed recovery.
________________


Focus on people, not addiction
Treat people as individuals.
Good
We treat the whole person, not just their substance misuse.
Every recovery journey is unique.
Treatment is designed around your individual needs.
Avoid
Addicts require structured intervention.
________________


Recovery is a journey
Talk about recovery as a process rather than a cure.
Good
Recovery doesn't end after treatment.
We're here to support you every step of the way.
Our aftercare services help you maintain long-term sobriety.
Avoid
Treatment fixes addiction.
